超过容器宽度，flex-grow不生效

item1 40%
item2 40%
item3 20% flex-grow: 1 不生效

一个元素设置flex-grow 占满

item1 flex-grow: 1
item2 flex-grow: 0
item3 flex-grow: 0

按照比例设置flex-grow 按比例均分

item1 flex-grow: 1
item2 flex-grow: 2
item3 flex-grow: 1

按照比例设置flex-grow 按小数比例均分 依旧占不满

item1 flex-grow: 0.1 grow长度为: 超出长度*0.1
item2 flex-grow: 2 grow长度为: 超出长度*0.2
item3 flex-grow: 1 grow长度为: 超出长度*0.1
